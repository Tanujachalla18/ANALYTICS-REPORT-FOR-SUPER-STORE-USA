# ANALYTICS-REPORT-FOR-SUPER-STORE-USA
Superstore Sales Analytics Dashboard is an end-to-end Tableau project that analyzes retail sales across regions, categories, customer segments, and time periods. It transforms raw data into interactive dashboards with KPI tracking, sales trends, profit analysis, and business insights to support data-driven decision-making.
Project Workflow
Data Preparation
Imported and structured the Superstore sales dataset.
Performed data validation and formatting.
Created calculated fields and business metrics for analysis.
Dashboard Development

Designed an interactive Tableau dashboard with dynamic filters and visualizations to explore business performance from multiple perspectives.
Key Dashboard Features
📈 Total Sales KPI
💰 Profit Margin Analysis
🔄 Return Rate Tracking
📦 Sales by Category
🛒 Sales by Sub-Category
🌎 State-wise Sales Analysis (Interactive Map)
👥 Sales by Customer Segment
🏆 Top 5 Best-Selling Products
📊 Monthly Sales Trend Analysis
🎛 Interactive Filters for Region, Year, and Metrics
Business Insights

The dashboard helps businesses to:

Monitor overall sales performance
Identify the most profitable product categories
Compare sales across different states and regions
Analyze customer segments and buying patterns
Track monthly sales growth and seasonal trends
Identify top-performing products
Measure return rates and profit margins
Support strategic business decisions through interactive reporting
